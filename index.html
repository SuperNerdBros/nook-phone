NookPhone

Animal Crossing: New Horizons Cozy Companion App & NookOS

Welcome to NookPhone.app, the ultimate companion tool for Animal Crossing: New Horizons. Running on NookOS, this web application helps you plan and manage your island life getaway package.

NookOS Utility Suite

  • Camera - Custom screenshot filters & photo frames
  • Nook Miles - Tracker for milestones & achievements
  • Critterpedia - Logs for bugs, fish, & sea creatures
  • DIY Recipes - Recipes calculator & materials list
  • Nook Shopping - Bells-based catalog ordering
  • Happy Island Designer - Layout planner & terraforming map
  • Custom Designs - Pixel art drawing pattern codes
  • Contacts - Villagers friendship logs & birthday calendars
  • Chat & Best Friends DMs - Resident multiplayer messaging
  • Rescue Service - Kiosk maps routing
  • Changelog - System updates release notes
